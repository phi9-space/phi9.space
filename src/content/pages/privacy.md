## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how phi9.space collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the website, contact us, or interact with our public research and data surfaces.

We keep collection narrow, use information for operational reasons, and avoid turning the site into an advertising or behavioral tracking surface.

## Information we collect

We may collect information you choose to provide, including your name, email address, organization, and any message you send through contact or collaboration workflows.

We may also collect limited technical information needed to operate the site, including browser details, device metadata, approximate location inferred from IP address, referring pages, and aggregate usage events.

If you engage with research, data, or product workflows that require additional information, the relevant surface will provide its own context and request only what is necessary for that interaction.

## How we use information

We use information to operate the website, respond to inquiries, understand usage patterns, improve our public materials, maintain security, and support legitimate research or collaboration workflows.

We may also use information to communicate about ongoing conversations, demos, partnerships, or updates you explicitly asked to receive.

## Analytics and cookies

We may use privacy-conscious analytics and essential cookies to understand how the site is used and to keep core functionality working.

We do not use the site for cross-site advertising, data brokerage, or behavioral profiling.

You can control cookie behavior through your browser settings, though some basic functionality may be affected if essential cookies are disabled.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with infrastructure, analytics, or communications providers that help us operate the site, subject to appropriate confidentiality and processing controls. We may also disclose information if required by law or when necessary to protect the security and integrity of our systems.

## Retention

We retain information only for as long as it is reasonably necessary for operational, legal, or security purposes.

If information is no longer needed, we delete it or reduce it to an anonymized or aggregate form where appropriate.

## Your choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your personal information.

To exercise those rights, or to ask how a specific workflow handles information, contact us at [research@phi9.space](mailto:research@phi9.space).

## Changes

We may update this policy as the website and its public surfaces evolve. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of the page.

## Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to [research@phi9.space](mailto:research@phi9.space).
